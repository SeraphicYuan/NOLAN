## Video direction

- **Look:** Highlighter Editorial (Vox-lineage) per frame.md — PAPER register (mist/parchment, ink-deep sentence-case Libre Franklin, Lora-italic asides, Inter label chrome) for argument and data; FOOTAGE register (full-bleed NOLAN krea2 grounds + bottom scrim, footage-text statements lower-left) for the concrete world. ONE highlighter-yellow mark per scene — a block sweep on the operative clause or an underline under the takeaway numeral — always landing ON the spoken cue, always ink-deep text on yellow.
- **Motion doctrine:** the sweep IS the word-sync (highlight/underline scaleX from left on cue — the asr-keyword-glow moment made physical); statements rise in per line, calm (kinetic-beat-slam at low amplitude — no per-word slamming; this system is composed, not shouty); numerals count up (`counting-dynamic-scale`); charts DRAW (strokes left→right, `stat-bars-and-fills` / path-draw mechanics, dot marker landing on the takeaway); a slow bounded camera drift (`multi-phase-camera` drift ≤6px/1.4% scale) under everything; FOOTAGE grounds get a slow Ken-Burns (scale 1.03→1.09, linear, fromTo). Transforms/opacity only, deterministic, seek-safe; no CSS transitions, no repeat/yoyo.
- **Pacing:** reveal on the voiceover cue across each scene's full window; the quote scene (F2 S4) is deliberately held and quiet. No front-loading; no mid-frame exits (only F2 S6 end-settles).
- **Type safety:** Libre Franklin + Lora + Inter via Google Fonts `@import` (render host has network). Sentence case display. Keep every element above y≈896px for the caption band.
