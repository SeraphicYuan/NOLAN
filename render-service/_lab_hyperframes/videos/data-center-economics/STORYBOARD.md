## Video direction

- **Asset blend (NOLAN bridge):** six concrete beats carry a full-bleed photographic ground (NOLAN krea2, "Dark Moody Atmosphere" — dark slate + amber, palette-matched to ink/fire-orange), mounted as a dedicated `class="clip"` ground layer under the type, dimmed 45–65% with a left-heavier scrim where statements sit, and a slow linear Ken-Burns (scale 1.03→1.10) across the scene window. Abstract/stat beats (stat grids, the 400M→900M compare, the bar chart, the orange payoff, the jobs kicker) stay pure type — the mix is per-scene, not image-everything.
- **Look:** Broadside at frame scale — flat ink-black plane, cream text, a single fire-orange accent; massive lowercase Barlow-900 as graphic primitive; IBM Plex Mono uppercase chrome (kickers, labels, `/` bullet, axis). Zero shadow, zero radius (save none here), 1px hairlines only. One register per Scene; Scene 1-5 of beat 1 and all of beat 2 are DARK, Frame 1 Scene 5 is the single ORANGE payoff.
- **Motion doctrine:** numbers are the show — count them up (`counting-dynamic-scale`) so the figure earns its size; percentages get a bar (`stat-bars-and-fills`, transforms only). Statements slam in per word (`kinetic-beat-slam`); the one inked clause per statement lights on its spoken word (`asr-keyword-glow`). Under everything, a slow, bounded camera drift (a few px / a hair of scale) keeps the flat plane alive — never a bounce, never a loop that reads as motion for its own sake. Transforms/opacity only; deterministic and seek-safe; no CSS transitions, no yoyo/repeat-driven state.
- **Pacing:** reveal each Scene on its voiceover cue across the full duration; the dense stat grids are the exception to the ~45-55% silence rule. No front-loading, no mid-frame exits (only Frame 2 Scene 6 may end-settle).
- **Type safety:** fonts are Barlow + IBM Plex Mono via Google Fonts `@import` (render host has network — GSAP loads from CDN); Latin/digits only in mono chrome. Keep every element above y≈896px (83% of 1080) for the caption pill.
