# Frame design — Highlighter Editorial (Vox-inspired)

canvas: #F1F3F2

## Palette
- highlighter: #FFF200   (the single accent — sweeps, underlines, marks)
- ink: #2B2D2C           (display + numerals)
- slate: #4C4E4D         (labels, kickers, supporting)
- mist: #F1F3F2          (paper ground)

## Type
- display: "Libre Franklin", weights 800/900 — hero words, numerals, statements
- label: "Inter", weights 500/600 — kickers, labels, small caps
- serif accent: "Lora" italic — supporting captions

## Feel
Editorial, high-contrast, ink-on-mist. One accent only (highlighter yellow), used sparingly
on the operative word / the takeaway number. Generous margins, left-anchored. Motion is calm
and motivated — count-ups, single sweeps, a settling camera — never decorative.
