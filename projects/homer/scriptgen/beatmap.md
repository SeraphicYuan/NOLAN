# Beat-map — Homer / The Odyssey  (constitution = channel-great-books-explained/style_guide.md)

## Spine + arc
**Angle (CHOSEN):** The Odyssey is a homecoming poem authored by the one Greek who never comes home to us — a blind singer, maybe not even one man, forever just over the horizon. *Odysseus : Ithaca :: Homer : us.* The master contrast: the wanderer who finally gets home vs. the poet who never does (sight/blindness, presence/absence, the man vs. the voice). Biographical determinism, done the only honest way Homer allows: the missing life IS the theme, and the poem hands us the author's self-portrait in the blind bard Demodocus.

**Arc ridden (guide's beat sequence):** Hook → Thesis/stakes → "Author biography" (here: the biography that isn't → Demodocus mirror) → Context (oral tradition) → Plot/character + analytical heart (the wanderer earns his myth) → the ground under the myth + the 200-year question (turn) → Legacy folded into the emotional back third (recognition & homecoming) → contemplative "unknowing" close.

**Retention rules imported (HARD / SKELETON):** hook in first 1–3 sentences → pivot to work + "life" within two sentences; braid, never chapter; alternate long analytical sentences with short punches; "we" not "you" (reserve "you" for one moral beat); quotes as pivots; reframe "flaws" (repetition, length) as genius; leave the central question OPEN; contemplative close on mystery/"unknowing"; NO numbered signposting, NO isolated analysis-only or biography-only beat, NO plot-summary open, NO hype CTA. Controversy (the forgery debate) deployed AS the hook + ONE accelerated fact-cluster, NEVER an argue-it-out lecture. Target ~1200 words (8.0 min).

## The "biography" that isn't → Demodocus as the author-mirror · pace:d · covers:[S1,S4] · serves-spine: The guide's biographical-determinism spine, adapted to a vanished life — we cash out the ABSENCE. No reliable biography, maybe not one man; so Homer painted his own portrait inside Book 8. ## Evidence: the wanderer earns his myth · pace:a · covers:[S1,S2,S4] · serves-spine: The hero half of the contrast, braided to blindness — the blind poet's hero triumphs by INNER sight, by cunning over force, and his one failure is a boast (naming himself). · devices: contrast (metis vs. bia — wits vs. brute glory; Odysseus vs. Achilles); CAPITALIZED vocal stress ("not by looking but by SEEING"); irony (the "Nobody" trick; the fatal boast); short punches on the trick; quantification (a sixth of the poem; ten more years) · anchors: monsters appear ONLY in Books 9–12, only inside the story Odysseus tells — a sixth of the poem [S4]; feigned-madness plow-and-salt test, baby Telemachus in the furrow, as the first proof of cunning [S1]; gives his name as "Nobody," blinds Polyphemus (son of Poseidon) with a sharpened stake, rides out under a ram [S1,S2]; Sirens survived by wax-in-ears + lashed to the mast [S1,S2]; Athena's help "impossible to tell apart from Odysseus's own racing mind" [S4]; the drunk-on-victory boast → Poseidon's curse → "ten more years lost at sea" [S1,S2].

## Turn: the ground under the myth + the 200-year question · pace:a→d · covers:[S3] · serves-spine: The controversy as an accelerated cluster, NOT a debate; then decelerate into the steelman reframe that unresolved-ness is not a hole but the poem wearing the poet's face. · devices: quantification (1873, 1952, 1795, "two hundred years"); irony (the merchant-digger vs. forgotten Calvert); in-beat rebuttal / steelman held on the same line; analogy/allusion (rival manuscripts of a classic ≠ a fake); contrast (scholar admits complexity / conspiracy-monger flattens it) · anchors: Schliemann's 1873 "Priam's gold" at Hisarlik — but Calvert identified the site first and history forgot him [S3]; Ventris cracks Linear B in 1952 → the Bronze-Age world had "palaces, kings, and writing" [S3]; Wolf's 1795 Prolegomena turned Homer "from an object of worship into a two-hundred-year academic question" [S3]; the honest verdict — the poems sit "between myth, literature, and memory"; "a tangled history of versions is not a forgery."

Slow into the human core, then braid to spine — Odysseus is RECOGNIZED and let home, the exact gift the poem withholds from its maker. · devices: contrast (Odysseus/Penelope vs. Agamemnon/Clytemnestra — the dark mirror); direct quotation / concrete sensory detail as pivot; deceleration; short declarative landing; "known by what only love could know" (aphoristic close of a movement) · anchors: twenty years — ten of war, ten lost at sea [S1,S2]; Penelope's shroud woven by day, un-woven by night, stalling 100+ suitors three years [S1,S2]; the old nurse Eurycleia feels the boar-scar on his thigh, the bronze basin crashing [S1]; the marriage bed built around a living olive tree, "one would have to cut through the tree itself" to move it [S1]; Agamemnon, home fast and murdered, as the dark mirror [S4].
