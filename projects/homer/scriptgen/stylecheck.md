## Do / Don't — confirmations

**Do**
- Hook opens on the shocking/unfamiliar (a poet nobody can find, now called a forgery) and grounds in the poem within ~two sentences. ✓
- Biography braided to text — but here the *absence* of biography is cashed out as Demodocus, and every later fact ("sung not written," the archaeology) is tied back to why the author dissolves. ✓
- Long/short alternation; "we" as shared journey; verbatim quotes as pivots; "flaws" (repetition, length) reframed as genius; competing readings (did one man write it?) left OPEN; reverent, never sneering; contemplative "unknowing" close. ✓

**Don't**
- No plot-summary open ✓ · no explicit word definitions ✓ · no theory jargon (avoided "oral-formulaic," used "machinery of memory") ✓ · no numbered signposting ✓ · symbol/question not resolved into one answer ✓ · no debunking of the classic ✓ · no over-"you" ✓ · no hype CTA or flat recap at the close ✓ · no isolated biography-only or analysis-only beat ✓.
