# Run Report — Homer scriptgen (v3, ab/v3)

## Thesis of this run
The **style guide is the constitution.** It encodes the channel's proven retention pattern *and* implies the *type* of spine its videos are built on. v3's job was to infer that spine type first, then thread a **resonant** spine of that type *through* the guide's structure — never reorganize the guide, and never let the mandated counter-source become the spine.

## Inferred spine TYPE
From the guide's Narrative Structure ("Author biography… *this is the spine*"), the braiding rule (biographical determinism / mirroring), the master device (a central contrast), and the Hook Patterns:

> **A single felt human through-line — one life mirrored into the work, organized around one emotional contrast — carried as a story a viewer *feels*, never as an intellectual debate.** The spine is always *a person and a longing*, not *a question about the text.*

## Chosen angle
**[CHOSEN] Home over glory — the first great story that says being *known* by the people you love beats being a legend.** (Resonance 5 · Evidence 5 · Style-fit 5.)
It is a felt human through-line built on the guide's master contrast (glory <-> home); it lands the mandated contemplative close on the olive-tree bed; and it *rescues* the biographical-mirroring move rather than abandoning it — since no single Homer can be found, the unfindable author becomes the final turn: *the poem about a man fighting to be known outlived the poet we can't name.*

## Use of the S3 forgery material
**Hook + one accelerated fact-cluster — never a lecture beat, never the spine.** The forgery charge opens the video as the sanctioned SHOCK hook (Beat 1), converts within two sentences into a *human* question, and is answered inside the essay's own momentum: the oral-tradition cluster (Wolf, formulas, Demodocus — Beat 3) and the archaeology cluster (Schliemann/Calvert/Troy, Linear B/Ventris 1952 — Beat 4). Both braid straight out of the spine; there is no standalone "is it real?" section. This is exactly S3's own move (version-complexity != forgery).

## Retention-curve note (how the hook + pacing follow the guide)
Open on a curiosity spike (guide Hook type 3 = shock/controversy) in the first 15 seconds, then **immediately trade scholarship for feeling** so the viewer is chasing an emotion, not a debate. The middle stays kinetic with two *accelerated* fact-clusters (facts/lists per the Pacing rule) braided into the emotional line — this is where a dry authenticity-debate version stalls, and where v3 keeps momentum. The whole **back third is a long decelerate** — Nobody -> glory-vs-home synthesis -> the reunion — so the curve *rises into feeling* at the exact 60-80% mark where retention usually sags, closing on the olive-tree bed and the vanished-poet turn (guide's contemplative close + modern-relevance + image-of-unknowing). No isolated analysis beats, no numbered signposting (DON'Ts honored).

## Difference from a dry authenticity-debate version (e.g. ab/new)
The earlier run made "the *Odyssey* has no author / is it a forgery?" the **structural spine** — a question, delivered as meta-analysis. v3 keeps that material (honestly, in the hook and one fast cluster) but makes the spine a **human longing to come home and be known** — so the same evidence now *serves feeling* instead of replacing it. Right spine *type*, higher resonance, guide left un-reorganized.
