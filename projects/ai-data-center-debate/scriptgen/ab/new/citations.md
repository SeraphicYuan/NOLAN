> "Median property values are north of $700,000."

**Scale of the boom [S1]**
> "Giant warehouses are popping up across the US, more than two every week."
> "We reached a total of [1],240 data centers. That's nearly four times the number in 2010."
> "could soon require as much as 12% of total US electricity use. In 2023, it was just over 4%."

**Power / grid cost [S1]**
