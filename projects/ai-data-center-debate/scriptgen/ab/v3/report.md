  pivoted within two sentences to the work + the fair question — guide Hook Patterns #1/#3.

  timeline -> thought-experiment) -> both-sides weighing -> standard sign-off. Braided, not chaptered;
  no numbered signposting; no isolated analysis-only beat.

  degree"; HEDGED honestly ("on paper," "practically speaking," "almost certainly"); the counter-
  source deployed as hook + accelerated fact-cluster, never as a lecture; Jevons labeled and reasoned.
- **Pacing:** accelerate on scale/water/money clusters; decelerate for the bedroom-noise humanizing,
