I never wanted to be an NPC. You know the type — running on a script someone else wrote. Same loop, every day, until it's over.

---

I wanted the opposite. Max out every stat. Mind, body, spirit, relationships, money.

---

A level 100 player. Every area of the map unlocked. Not jacked in one place — jacked everywhere. Bank overflowing with gold.

---

So I chased it. As a teenager I got obsessed with fitness. Then I wanted to absorb every bit of knowledge I could find. Then I wanted to be free, so I failed at business after business until one finally worked.

---

Fifteen years digging through psychology, philosophy, money, religion, startups, the internet. Five years writing it all down in public. And the same patterns kept showing up, over and over.

---

Those patterns became one thing. HUMAN 3.0. One map to escape mediocrity and actually reach your potential.

---

Now, it's not like there aren't maps out there already. There are tons of them.

---

Spiral Dynamics for the mind. Buddhism and Christianity for meaning. Red Pill and feminism for dating. eCommerce and consulting for money. Every guru, every model.

---

And they all hold real truths. But every single one has the same flaw. It's locked to one domain of life.

---

Here's the problem. Knowledge isn't a stack of separate classes. It's a web. We just got taught math, English, and science in little sealed boxes, so we think life works that way too.

---

So you end up with the spiritual teacher who has a frail body. The businessman who can't hold a relationship. The alpha male who's an emotional wreck. It's rare that anyone is truly developed across the board.

---

And the models that do connect a few domains, like the old Greek philosophies? Most of them predate the internet, predate AI, and barely touch money — the thing that runs most people's entire lives.
