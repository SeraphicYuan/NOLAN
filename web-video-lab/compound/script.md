Einstein supposedly called it the eighth wonder of the world. Compound interest.

---

The idea is simple. You earn interest. Then you earn interest on the interest. And the growth starts feeding itself.

---

A thousand dollars at eight percent becomes two thousand in nine years. Four thousand in eighteen. Ten thousand in thirty.

---

Three things decide where you land. Time. Rate. And how consistent you stay.

---

But it cuts both ways. Debt compounds against you in exactly the same way.

---

So start early. Stay in. And let time do the heavy lifting.
