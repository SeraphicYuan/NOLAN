Project Data

Give a composition its source data — a provenance-stamped table a data-viz block can bind (chart, pie, stat, sankey…), or an ingested paper/scan a document scene can zoom & highlight. Both are provenance-gated: no source, no register.

📊 Datasets — CSV / JSON tables, bound by dataset+query+encode

Columns & dtypes are inferred from the header.

📄 Documents — PDF / image, bound by a document scene (region zoom, highlight)

A PDF keeps its text layer (region ids to target); an image scan becomes a page you target by find phrase or explicit rect.
