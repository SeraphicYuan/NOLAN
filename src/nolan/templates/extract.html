Extract Assets from a Link

Paste a page URL — NOLAN pulls the highest-definition images it can find. Site-aware parsers (Gutenberg, Wikimedia Commons, The Met, Internet Archive, Library of Congress, IIIF manifests) plus a universal HTML fallback (linked full-res over thumbnails, srcset, og:image).

Preview lists what was found without downloading. Extract & Download saves full-res files to .scratch/extracted/<host> with a manifest, and (when → Picture Library is checked) ingests them into the searchable library.
